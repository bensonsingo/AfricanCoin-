Charity Initiatives with OliveTree Investments

Charity partnership with OliveTree Investments, we are committed to addressing critical challenges in underdeveloped African countires by using Crypto. Our charity initiatives will focus on essential sectors such as clean water, food security, education, renewable energy, and financial inclusion, among others. Here’s how we’re making a difference:

  • Clean Water & Water Resources: We will invest in projects that improve access to clean drinking water, reduce water scarcity, and ensure sustainable water management practices.
  • Food Security & Agricultural Activities: OliveTree will support sustainable agriculture and food processing projects, helping local communities with the tools, training, and resources they need to thrive.
  • Education: We will invest in local education programs, providing the necessary infrastructure and resources to uplift young generations with knowledge and skills for a brighter future.
  • Solar Energy: Focused on renewable energy solutions, we will implement solar energy projects to provide affordable electricity to communities, helping reduce dependency on fossil fuels.
  • Real Estate & Building Techniques: Our investments will include affordable housing projects and sustainable building techniques that improve living conditions and provide long-term benefits to local economies.
  • Technical & Financial Services: OliveTree will provide access to financial services, promoting entrepreneurship and empowering local businesses through digital tools and education.
  • Internet Connectivity: We aim to improve internet access in remote areas by providing mobile connectivity and Internet of Things (IoT) solutions, empowering communities with better opportunities for growth and education.

All of these initiatives will be supported by the funds generated from transactions within AfricanCoin . As the price of African Coin increases, we will allocate a portion of the transaction fees to fund these impactful projects in counties with poor conditions and those suffering from high inflation. The rising value of our ecosystem will directly help tackle these challenges by driving investments and facilitating infrastructure development, offering a brighter and more sustainable future for the most vulnerable populations.

Overview

AfricanCoin (ACN) is a decentralized cryptocurrency developed to serve as a digital asset for African economies. Built on the Ethereum blockchain using the ERC-20 standard, AfricanCoin is designed to bridge financial gaps in Africa and support economic growth through blockchain technology.

The AfricanCoin ecosystem aims to revolutionize the way financial transactions are conducted by offering a stable and secure digital currency that caters to both individuals and businesses across the African continent.

Vision

AfricanCoin (ACN) is a digital asset that allows you to store your cash securely in coins, offering a smart way to safeguard and grow your wealth. It’s not just a coin—it’s an opportunity to transform your savings into a digital asset that can appreciate over time.

With AfricanCoin, you can store your cash in coins, watching its value increase as the cryptocurrency market grows. You can also easily transfer crypto, giving you the flexibility to manage your money across borders and participate in the global digital economy.

By holding AfricanCoin, you’re not just protecting your funds—you’re taking part in Africa’s rise in the cryptocurrency world, helping to boost the continent’s adoption of digital finance while securing your financial future.

Features

Secure and Decentralized

Built on the Ethereum blockchain, AfricanCoin offers a decentralized solution to financial transactions, ensuring security and transparency for all users.

Affordable and Accessible

With a low transaction fee and an easy-to-use wallet interface, AfricanCoin is designed to be accessible to everyone, from individuals to businesses.

Token Release Mechanism

Tokens are released periodically according to a pre-defined schedule. Automatic releases ensure that the supply is distributed fairly and efficiently.

Community Support and Growth

AfricanCoin aims to foster a community-driven ecosystem, where influencers, investors, and users play an active role in the growth and development of the coin.

Tokenomics

Total Supply

The total supply of AfricanCoin is capped at 149 million ACN tokens. The breakdown of the supply is as follows:

  • Total Public Release: 77 million tokens
  • Initial Public Release: 27.3 million tokens
  • Batch Release: 13 million tokens every 10 years
  • Max Purchase Limit: 250,000 coins per address and week

Batch Token Release Chart (Until 2070)

Allocations

Allocations are divided into several categories to ensure fair distribution and long-term sustainability:

  • African Exchange Allocation: 23 million tokens
  • ICO Investors Allocation: 14.4 million tokens
  • Team Allocation: 5.8 million tokens
  • Influencers Allocation: 5.8 million tokens
  • Founder Allocation: 9 million tokens
  • Liquidity Providers Allocation: 11.7 million tokens

Staking and Rewards

AfricanCoin supports staking, offering a 4% Annual Percentage Yield (APY) for stakers. Rewards are allocated from the staking pool, which accumulates from token distributions.
